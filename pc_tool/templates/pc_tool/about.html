What is this site for?

I've found that I regularly have lists of postcodes in excel format (1 postcode per cell, down a column, when I actually need them separated by commas. Wanting to learn the Django web framework, I thought this would be a good opportunity to completely over-engineer the solution.

How to use this site

Copy the column of postcodes in excel, open the site, click New, give the postcode list a title, paste the postcodes into the 'Text' field, and then hit Save. Then select the CSV box, select all and copy to clipboard to then use from there.
